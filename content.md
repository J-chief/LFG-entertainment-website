All user-facing copy for seeding the CMS. Voice: bold, short, confident, hype without cringe. Sentence fragments allowed. No corporate speak, no exclamation-mark spam.

## 1. Brand Voice Rules
- Short sentences. Punchy. Second person ("you").
- Sell the feeling, not the features. "The bass hits different" > "high-quality sound systems."
- Uppercase for headlines only. Never all-caps body text.
- Words we use: night, stage, crowd, sound, energy, inside, access.
- Words we ban: "utilize," "solutions," "world-class" (overused), "don't miss out."
